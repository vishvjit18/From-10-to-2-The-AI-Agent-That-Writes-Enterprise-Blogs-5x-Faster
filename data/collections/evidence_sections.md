## Practical Integration: Weaving AI into Your Writing Workflow

### Evidence for: Best practices for prompt engineering from AI research

**Research Summary Block: Effective Prompting for Creative Output**
AI research emphasizes that the quality of AI-generated creative output is highly dependent on the input prompt. Effective prompting involves clarity, specificity, and context. Studies suggest that providing detailed constraints (e.g., desired tone, character motivation, narrative genre) and using iterative refinement—asking the AI to expand, simplify, or alter its previous output—significantly improves relevance and creativity.

**Data Summary Bullets: Key Prompt Engineering Techniques**
*   **Specify Role:** Instruct the AI to act as a specific type of assistant (e.g., "Act as a fantasy novelist").
*   **Provide Context:** Include relevant background information about characters, plot, or setting.
*   **Define Output Format:** Request output in a specific structure (e.g., bullet points, dialogue format, descriptive paragraph).
*   **Use Constraints:** Set parameters for tone, style, length, and content (e.g., "Write in a noir style, under 150 words, focusing on internal monologue").
*   **Iterate and Refine:** Use follow-up prompts to guide the AI toward the desired outcome (e.g., "Make it more suspenseful," "Expand on the character's fear").

*   **Stereotypical Associations:** Models may disproportionately link certain jobs (e.g., nurse, teacher) with female pronouns and others (e.g., engineer, CEO) with male pronouns.

*   **Lack of Diverse Representation:** AI-generated characters or narratives might default to dominant cultural perspectives, lacking authentic representation of diverse experiences.
